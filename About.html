Description

Wichita State University's Dungeons and Dragons club meets every Sunday. It is run by current students and avid D&D players. It runs Adventurers Leauge styled games, and uses 5e books as a basis. Some homebrew is allowed. Snacks are used to purchase homebrew when in-person sessions begin!



Editions

It is based on Wizards of the Coast's Dungeons and Dragons. 5th Edition is the only edition run; other editions, including Pathfinder, are illegal for table play. This is because it exclusively runs Adventurers Leauge. Thank you for your understanding.



Players

New players are welcome to join. Preferably, a new player will join at a level-one session, as it is easiest to get a grasp on the game with the fewest mechanics possible. However, you are welcome to join the game at any time. Alumni are also welcome. It is also understood that sometimes players are uncomforatable with roleplaying, but it is always encouraged to keep conversation in-character and engaging! Nobody is here to judge!



Disclaimer

Please be patient and polite to other players, and respect your Dungeon Master. It is important to remember that your Dungeon Master is facilitating your fun, and improper treatment of fellow players or your DM is grounds for disiplinary action and a potential ban. Everybody is here to have fun, and that happens best when everyone cooperates and gets along.
