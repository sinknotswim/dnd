Races in DND

Races have become much more maliable now that Tasha's Cauldron of Everything (5e sourcebook) has been released. Therefore, the Races page will only cover a table of standard races and notable features.

It's important to keep in mind that races (and racism) is a very real-world issue that can be hard to touch on. However, this is a fantasy game, and players are encouraced to keep light; in D&D your skin can be devil-red or genasi-blue, and nobody cares.
